Mean

The average of elements in a set. Computed by summing the elements and dividing that sum by the number of elements. . Typically denoted by .

avg = mean([1 2 3 4])

avg =
                       2.5

Variance

A measure of the "spread" of data from the mean. Designated as , it is the sum of the squares of the difference in the observations from the means: .

variance = var([1 2 3 4])

variance =
          1.66666666666667

Standard Deviation

The square root of the variance, designated by .

stdex = std([1 2 3 4])

stdex =
          1.29099444873581
